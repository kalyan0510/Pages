The Satellite Rescue Mission

Satellites have tons of sensors to guide them to orient their equipment, like sun sensors, polaris star sensors, gyros and etcetera. Such sensors are used in critical tasks like orienting solar panels to charge onboard batteries and like orienting communication antennas. Any failure in these sensors can lead to the demise of the satellite. Lets check if we can rescue a satellite with completely failed orientation sensors.
